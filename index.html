About Us

Trusted Eye Care
Since 2008

At Cana Optical Clinic, we provide exceptional eye care services to our community in Carmona, Cavite. Founded by Dr. Lalaine Cana, the clinic has grown from a single-doctor practice to a team of five licensed optometrists serving thousands of patients across Carmona and neighboring areas.

From routine eye examinations to precision prescription fitting and a wide selection of optical frames, every patient receives the highest standard of care in a warm, welcoming environment.
